Data Curation Profile Domain Profile

Data Curation Domain Profile (DCP) for the field of Cultural Heritage Preservation with a subfield of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Applications and Spatial Data Practices. Our goal was to deliver a dataset of 100 open access articles published between 2020 and 2025 accompanied by a DCP and to deposit our work in an appropriate repository. Made for IS262A Data Management and Practice Fall 2025.
